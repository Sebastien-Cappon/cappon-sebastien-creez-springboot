CRUD Endpoints

Persons list : http://local:8080/persons
Firestations list : http://local:8080/firestations
Medical records list : http://local:8080/medicalRecords

Person informations : http://localhost:8080/person/<first-name>-<last-name>
Firestation mapping : http://localhost:8080/firestation/<address>
Medical record details : http://localhost:8080/medicalRecord/<first-name>-<last-name>

URL Endpoints examples

List of households covered by a firestation : http://localhost:8080/firestation?stationNumber=<station-number>
List of phone numbers a firestation must call : http://localhost:8080/phoneAlert?firestation=<station-number>
List of children and their relatives by household : http://localhost:8080/childAlert?address=<address>
Lists of residents and firestations by address : http://localhost:8080/fire?address=<address>
Lists of households by firestations : http://localhost:8080/flood/stations?stations=<stations-list>
Other way to get person informations : http://localhost:8080/personInfo?firstName=<first-name>&lastName=<last-name>
List of email addresses by city : http:///localhost:8080/communityEmail?city=<city>
